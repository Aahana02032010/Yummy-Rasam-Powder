Bramhins Tilisaaru Powder l Yummy Rasam Powder

Rasam Powder is an easy and simple multipurpose spice mix or masala recipe specifically used to make rasam or saaru. There are myriad variations to the south indian rasam and the spice mix masala differs as per the rasam recipe. This recipe post specifically describes the udupi or south canara based saaru or rasam powder which can be used for various reasons.

RECIPE


Let's Start:


INGREDIENTS:

For Powder:

1.Dhaniya - 1 tbsp
2.Jeera(Cumin Seeds)- 1/2 tbsp
3.Meethi Seeds - 4 tsp
4.Mustard - 4 tbsp
5.Guntoor Chillie - 15
6.Byadige Chillie - 15

For rasam:

1.Toor Dal - 1 tbsp
2.Tomato - 2 to 3 (Any: Jam Tomato or Country Tomato)
3. Coriander - 1 tbsp
4.Curry Leaves - 1 tbsp
5. Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
6.Salt - to taste
7. Tamarind Extract - 2 to 3 tsp
8.Jaggery - 1 tsp
9. Oil - 1tsp


Method:

For Powder:

1. Fry all the ingredients together if you are making little. If you are doing more then fry them seperately.
2. Powder all the ingredients together if you are making little. But make sure you powder them together.

For Rasam:

1. Boil 1tbsp Toor Dal and 2 to 3 tomatoes and mix well.
2. Boil the cut coriander and put the curry leaves with turmeric and salt with the tomatoes and dal.
3.Put the tamarind extract and jagerry and mix it with the ingredients.
4. Boil them together and put 3 to 4 spoons of RASAM PUDI prepared.
